Public or Private Bookmarks and Collections¶

Public or Private Bookmarks and Collections feature lets you define whether bookmarks and collections are visible to all or only to certain users.

Description:
  • Public bookmarks are intended for all users of the system.
  • Only the user who set a bookmark as private can access that bookmark.
  • A locked icon indicates that a bookmark is a private one.
Related topics:
  • Search and Store Bookmarks
Application:

  • Use a private bookmark for securing access to a personal product watchlist.

  • Share your product watchlist by using a public bookmark.

  • Example:

    Suppose the marketing team of a company has created a bookmark for a trial, so they make the bookmark as a private bookmark because they don’t want to show their bookmark to the other users of the system. On the other hand, if they want to share the bookmark with other users, they keep the bookmark as a public bnookmark.

How to do: Public or Private Bookmarks and Collections¶

This How To guide provides instructions on how to define a bookmark (or collection) to either Public or Private. A public setting grants access to all users while a private setting grants access only to specific users. To do this, follow these steps:

Bookmarks: Setting a Public Bookmark¶


  1. Log in as admin.


  1. Click the Bookmark icon and go to the desired bookmark.

  1. The default setting of a bookmark is Public. Keep this setting to make a public bookmark.


  1. Once done, the bookmark can be viewed by all users.

As seen on the image above, the bookmark can be viewed by the Seller.



Bookmarks: Setting a Private Bookmark¶


  1. Log in as admin.

  1. Click the Bookmark icon and go to the desired bookmark.

  1. Set the bookmark to Private.


  1. Once done, only certain users can view the bookmark.

As seen on the image above, the bookmark cannot be viewed by the seller as it is restricted to the admin.



Collections: Setting a Public Collection¶


  1. To start creating a collection, select the products or assets that will be included in the collection.


  1. Click on the pin icon on the right side of the search bar to create the collection.


  1. Enter the name of the collection and click Create.


  1. Once created, search for the collection as you would a bookmark.

  1. Once found, check that the default setting is Public. Keep this setting for a public collection.


  1. Once done, the collection can be viewed by all users.


Collections: Setting a Private Collection¶


  1. To set a collection to private, search for the collection.

  1. Once searched, click on the lock icon to change the setting to Private.


  1. Once done, only certain users can view the collection.

As seen on the images above, the collection cannot be viewed by the seller as it is restricted to the admin.
