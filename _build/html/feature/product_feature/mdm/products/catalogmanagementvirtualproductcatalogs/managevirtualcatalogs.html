Manage Virtual CatalogsÂ¶

Manage Virtual Catalogs feature creates multiple collections and bookmarks to effectively manage different selections of products in the catalog.

Description:
  • A Virtual Catalog is a collection of various items put together by a user (usually a retailer) to be used at a specific time or for a certain occasion.
  • These catalogs vary according to users and can be restricted in terms of who can view and edit them.
Related topics:
  • Collections and Bookmarks
Application:

  • A retailer uses a Virtual Catalog to create his own collection of products for special occasions.

  • Catalogs can be made user-specific.

  • Example:

    Retailer A with a clothing franchise creates a winter collection in a catalog and uses this virtual catalog when winter arrives.

  • Note: The collection created is not in PIM but in the entity, Virtual Catalog.
